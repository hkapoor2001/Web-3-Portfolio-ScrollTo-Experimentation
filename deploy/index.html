The Project:

.net experimental publication

NET. is an experimental publication that explores the history of net art, an Avant-Garde art movement that began in the 1990s and used the internet as its primary vehicle of communication. NET. is inspired by the presentation of prominent artwork in the movement, which explored both fear and excitement at the advent and ramifications of the internet. Using a Raspberry Pi and LCD display housed in a plexiglass casing, NET. includes a physical video component inspired by “Every Icon” by John F Simon Jr’s extension of the medium into physical space. This video component features data-moshing, a technique of data corruption and video manipulation.

The Inspiration:

The conceptual process for Net. began with inspiration from Every Icon, an artwork created by John F Simon Jr. The piece consists of a 32 x 32 grid of black and white squares. It progresses through combinations of squares until every square is black, eventually creating every possible image. Each line takes exponentially longer to complete, and it will eventually be complete after several hundred trillion years. It exists as online installation, a Palm Pilot application, and a self-contained wall hanging. As a self contained object, Every icon exemplifies a physical extension of the net art movement into real spaces, reflecting the shared cultural understandings surrounding the internet during the 1990s.

The Project:

appel farm branding

Appel Farm Arts and Music Center is a nonprofit arts center based in Elmer, New Jersey focused on arts education. It offers an overnight summer camp that educates students aged 7-17 in Art, Music and Theater, educational programs for field trips and arts workshops, Site rentals and South Jersey Arts Fest. Appel Farm differentiates itself through its dedication to the community and its core values of individual creativity. A problem I found with Appel Farm's current brand is the lack of clarity in Appel farm's different sub-brands and offerings. To solve this, I consolidated the different aspects of Appel Farm Arts and Music Center under one brand: Appel Farm. I then created the Appel Farm Arts Camp and Appel Farm arts and Music campus brands to help organize the different aspects of Appel Farm.

The Project:

the nesting house branding

The Nesting House provides gently used items for babies and small children for affordable prices through consignment. The Nesting House differentiates itself by providing a boutique experience in order to encourage the re-use of items. The goal of this project was to create a series of logos and branding materials to communicate The Nesting house's brand values of sustainability and nurturing.

The Project:

Wes Anderson Typographic Poster

This map poster explores typography through pivotal movies in director Wes Anderson’s film career. Through the use of collaged imagemaking and color coded texture, this poster allows the viewer to navigate a whollistic depiction of Wes Anderson’s use of type.
